Embark on a Goa Adventure

Day 1: Embarking on the Goa Adventure

  • Late Night Departure: Roll out of Bangalore by 11 PM, buzzing with excitement for the journey ahead.
  • We'll coordinate five convenient pickup locations (examples: Indiranagar Metro Station, Koramangala Forum Mall, Majestic Bus Stand) to gather everyone for the overnight journey.

Day 2: Sun, Sand, and Settling In

  • Arrival and Resort Check-in: As the sun paints the Goan sky, we'll arrive at the vibrant Ginger Tree Boutique Resort around 11 AM. Settle in, freshen up, and let's gather for a delicious lunch by 1 PM.
  • Morjim Beach Extravaganza: Feel the sand between your toes as we head to the captivating Morjim Beach. Unleash your inner beach bum until 6 PM – play games, build sandcastles, or simply soak up the serenity.
  • Anjuna Nightlife and Markets: After a delightful dinner back at the resort, we’ll delve into the electrifying nightlife of Anjuna Beach. Explore the bustling markets, their treasures shimmering under the moonlight.

Day 3: Watersports Frenzy and Goan Exploration

  • Rise and Shine: Start the day on an active note, waking up by 6 AM and getting ready for a thrilling day. Fuel up with a hearty breakfast at the resort by 8 AM.
  • Watersports Extravaganza: Get those adrenaline levels pumping! We'll embark on a full-day watersports adventure, conquering five exhilarating activities: scuba diving, banana boat rides, jet skiing, speed boating, and parasailing.
  • Goa at Your Leisure: Returning to the resort around 5-6 PM, the rest of the evening is yours to design. Discover the legendary pubs and beaches of Baga and Calangute – let your adventurous spirit guide you.

Day 4: Farewell to Goa, Memories in the Making

  • Leisurely Morning and Checkout: Enjoy a relaxed morning and savor your last breakfast at the resort (available until 10 AM). Pack your bags full of unforgettable experiences as we bid adieu to Ginger Tree Boutique Resort.
  • Poolside Celebration: Before departing, we'll throw an unforgettable pool party– a final splash of Goan magic to cherish!
  • Agoda Fort Exploration and Departure: Immerse yourselves in Goan history at the majestic Agoda Fort. As the sun sets, we'll board our bus back to Bangalore, hearts filled with the vibrant spirit of Goa.
